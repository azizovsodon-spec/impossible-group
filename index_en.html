5 steps to your first job

1

Choosing a direction

Do not try to learn everything at once. Choose one direction (e.g., Frontend) and focus on it for 2–3 months.

2

Basic syntax

Learn the basics: variables, loops, conditions. This is the foundation of any programming language.

3

First project (Portfolio)

Theory without practice is useless. Build your first calculator or To-Do list.

4

Working with Git

Learn to store your code on GitHub. It is your face to employers.

5

Internship and Job Offer

Start applying for jobs. Experience is more important than money.
